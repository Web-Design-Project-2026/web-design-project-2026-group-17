MAKEUP GUIDE

STEP 1 - SKINCARE (VERY IMOPORTANT)

  • Greate makeup starts with your skin.
  • Cleanse your face
  • Apply a hydrating serum
  • Use a rich moisturizer
  • Finish with SPF

Tip: Hydrated skin = smoother looking lines

STEP 2 - LIGHT BASE (FOUNDATION)

  • Choose a light to medium coverage foundation
  • Apply with a damp sponge or brush
  • Focus on the center of the face and blend outward

Tip: Avoid heaby layers, they can settle into fine lines

STEP 3 - CONCEALER (USE SPARINGLY)

  • Apply a small amount under the eyes and where needed
  • Use a creamy lightweight formula
  • Gently tap it in

STEP 4 - SET LIGHTLY

  • Use a small amount of translucent powder
  • Focus on the T-zone
  • Let the rest of your skin stay glowy

STEP 5 - BRONZER

  • Bronzer adds warmth and dimention
  • Choose a soft natural shade
  • Apply lightly

STEP 6 - BLUSH

  • Blush brings life back to the face
  • Choose soft shades
  • Blend upward towards the temples

STEP 7 - EYES

  • Use soft neutal shades
  • Avoid harsh lines
  • Blend well

STEP 8 - BROWS

  • Fill in sparse areas
  • Follow your natural shape
  • Brush upward

STEP 9 - LIPS

  • Use a lip liner
  • Choose creamy lipsticks
  • Avoid very matte formulas
